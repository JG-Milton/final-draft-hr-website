About Us

Established in 2008 by the Teng Family, Jaya Grocer is Malaysia's leading mass-premium supermarket chain, focusing on freshness and quality. Jaya Grocer offers fresh produce and a variety of grocery items from international and local brands, along with affordable staples.

From its first store in Plaza 33, Petaling Jaya, Jaya Grocer now operates over 50 stores, mainly in Klang Valley, reflecting strong demand for its unique grocery concept. Each store is designed for a pleasant shopping experience, with specialty sections catering to diverse dietary needs.

Jaya Grocer has partnered with Grab to offer on-demand grocery delivery, enhancing convenience with app-based solutions that bring the store to customers’ doorsteps. This strategic partnership highlights Jaya Grocer’s commitment to innovation.

Jaya Grocer’s VISION is to be the leading online-to-offline (O2O) retailer, providing a rewarding and exciting customer experience. Its MISSION is to offer high-quality, fresh produce at great value, supported by core VALUES: Growth, Trust, Teamwork, Customer Satisfaction, and Integrity.

With outlets in prime locations, Jaya Grocer focuses on quality, variety, and customer satisfaction, establishing itself as a trusted name in Malaysia's retail market.
